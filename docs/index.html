About Me

I'm a Software Engineer who wears whatever hat it takes to get things done—backend, frontend, DevOps, product. I recently completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science, where I mostly tried to stop distributed systems from falling apart (and drank a lot of tea trying to understand proofs).

Previously, I led engineering efforts at KLDiscovery, revamping a messy permissions system and building distributed platforms that handle 50k+ daily events—without crashing or crying.

These days, I’m all-in on DevSkill, a next-gen interview prep platform that simulates real interviews with structured, story-driven problem solving. No grind. Just growth.

I also built Reality Layer—a belief-analysis tool designed to challenge echo chambers and prompt reflection. Think cognitive antivirus powered by LLMs.

Some dream of a better world. I try to build it—one system, one prompt, and one stubborn IKEA shelf at a time.
